Training Routine

There multiple training splits to choose from:

Such as PPL, Full-body, Upper-lower.

The PPL training split means Push, Pull, Legs. Meaning you will workout the push muscles, then the next day will be the pull muscles then the next the leg muscles. The Full-body routine, is training the whole body, such as chest, arms, shoulders, back, legs. This training split is recommended to beginners. Lastly, Upper-lower routine is training the upper body one day then the lower body the next day. Remember you should train at least 3 to 4 times a week for good growth, the maximum is for most people is 6 times a week.
